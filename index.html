Billionaires Advisor is a trading and wealth management platform that simplifies investing,making it easy and affordable for the everyday investor to build wealth and achieve their financial goals by investing along billionaires and leading banks from a click of a button with no technical knowledge.

Our easy to use platform was built with a simple philosophy: everyone should have access to investing in a simplified manner. Thanks to fractional shares, anyone can invest in the stock market by easily choosing a billionaire, investment banks or pension funds, read their bio view of the stocks they own and see who to trade alongside.

Stock purchases/sales: All stock trades and portfolio data are taken directly from the investor’s SEC filings, namely Form 13F a very complex report, in which billionaires investors and banks are required to submit portfolio holdings within 45 days after the quarter end, and Schedule 13D, which requires investors who own at least 5% of a company to report changes in the holding within 10 days of the trade. While we try to make the information as accurate as possible, we cannot guarantee the accuracy of the information we publish.
